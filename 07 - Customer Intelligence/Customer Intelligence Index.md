# Customer Intelligence Index

Capture approved, useful relationship knowledge by company, region, team, and contact.

Track:

- relationship history and strategic importance
- communication and proposal preferences
- recurring project requirements
- contracting, billing, delivery, and documentation expectations
- response and approval patterns
- common issues and lessons learned
- commitments requiring follow-up

Use [[Customer Profile Template]]. Avoid unsupported judgments and restrict personal or sensitive information.
