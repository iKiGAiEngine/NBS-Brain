# Vendor Intelligence Index

Capture vendor capabilities and actual performance without mixing verified facts with opinion.

Track:

- product coverage and territory
- contacts and communication channels
- quote responsiveness and completeness
- pricing and freight terms
- lead-time accuracy
- order and acknowledgment accuracy
- damage, shortage, and warranty performance
- recurring risks and approved alternatives

Use [[Vendor Profile Template]]. Sensitive discounts and pricing must follow approved data-handling rules.
