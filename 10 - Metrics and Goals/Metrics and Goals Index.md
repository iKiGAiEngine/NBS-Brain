# Metrics and Goals Index

Metrics should support decisions rather than create reporting work without action.

## Initial Dimensions

- growth and backlog
- gross margin and cost leakage
- estimating cycle time, hit rate, and omissions
- vendor quote response and completeness
- project issue age and forecast accuracy
- purchasing cycle time and acknowledgment accuracy
- delivery completeness, timeliness, and damage
- invoice exceptions and processing time
- customer response and repeat work
- revenue and workload per employee
- automation adoption, savings, and prevented errors

Each metric must define its formula, source, owner, cadence, target, limitations, and action trigger.
