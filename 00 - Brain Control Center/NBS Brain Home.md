## Brain Standard

Every recommendation should identify:

- Recommended action
- Supporting facts
- Assumptions and missing information
- Risks and downstream effects
- Responsible owner
- Next action and deadline
- Required approval
- Confidence level

> The Brain is a decision-support system. It does not independently make contractual, financial, legal, personnel, or customer commitments.
