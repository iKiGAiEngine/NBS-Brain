# NBS Brain Build Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Foundation

- Company purpose and north star
- Operating principles
- Decision framework
- Governance and source-of-truth rules
- End-to-end process map

## Phase 2 — Operational Structure

- Create all core libraries and templates
- Define organization, roles, and ownership
- Document the opportunity-to-handoff workflow
- Track knowledge gaps and validation needs

## Phase 3 — Project Execution

- Buyout and budgeting
- Submittals and approvals
- Requisitions and purchase orders
- Vendor acknowledgments and lead-time control
- Delivery, changes, invoicing, forecasting, and closeout

## Phase 4 — Intelligence

- Product, vendor, and customer knowledge
- Metrics and exception dashboards
- Lessons-learned feedback loops
- Decision-support prompts and AI retrieval rules

## Phase 5 — AiPM Execution

- Convert approved processes into software requirements
- Automate routine work and surface exceptions
- Add permissions, audit logs, monitoring, and failure handling
- Measure adoption, savings, error reduction, and business value

Complete and validate [[Opportunity and Estimating Overview]].
