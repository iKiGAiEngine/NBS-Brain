# NBS Purpose and North Star

## Purpose

NBS exists to make commercial building-specialties procurement and project coordination more accurate, reliable, efficient, and profitable for NBS and its customers.

## North Star

NBS should become the preferred operating and procurement partner for Division 10 and related specialty materials by consistently delivering:

- accurate scope interpretation
- competitive and sustainable pricing
- dependable procurement and delivery coordination
- clear ownership and communication
- reduced project risk
- measurable value beyond material supply

## Long-Term Direction

NBS should grow through a repeatable, technology-supported operating system rather than through proportional increases in manual administration.

The operating model should:

- protect and improve gross margin
- reduce preventable errors and rework
- shorten response and cycle times
- preserve institutional knowledge
- standardize repeatable work
- surface exceptions early
- provide leadership with reliable operating information
- support growth without equal growth in confusion and overhead
