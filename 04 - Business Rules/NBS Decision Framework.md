# NBS Decision Framework

Evaluate recommendations in this order:

1. Safety, ethics, legal, and contractual requirements
2. Existing customer and vendor commitments
3. Protection of NBS from financial, operational, and reputational risk
4. Project success and schedule
5. Profitability, cash flow, and margin protection
6. Customer experience and relationship impact
7. Efficiency, repeatability, and scalability
8. Long-term strategic value and organizational learning

## Standard Recommendation Format

Every material recommendation should include:

- **Recommended action**
- **Reasoning**
- **Confirmed facts**
- **Assumptions and missing information**
- **Risks and alternatives**
- **Financial or operational impact**
- **Responsible owner**
- **Next action**
- **Deadline or trigger**
- **Confidence:** high, medium, or low
- **Required approval**

## Escalate When

- contractual language is unclear or conflicting
- cost or margin exposure exceeds approved authority
- customer direction changes scope, price, or schedule
- an approved product cannot be supplied as committed
- information from authoritative sources conflicts
- a decision affects legal, personnel, security, or restricted financial matters
- no accountable owner can be established
