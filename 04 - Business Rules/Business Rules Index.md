# Business Rules Index

Business rules are mandatory requirements, limits, tolerances, approval thresholds, calculations, or escalation triggers. They must identify an owner, evidence source, approval state, effective date, and affected processes.

## Initial Rule Groups

- Bid qualification and assignment
- Proposal review and approval
- Markup, margin, tax, freight, and escalation
- Quote validity and substitution handling
- Purchasing authority and PO changes
- Invoice matching tolerances
- Change management and cost recovery
- Data ownership, permissions, and audit requirements

Use [[Business Rule Template]]. Draft rules must not be presented as approved policy.
