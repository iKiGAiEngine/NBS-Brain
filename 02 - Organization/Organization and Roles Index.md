# Organization and Roles Index

## Purpose

Define who owns decisions, processes, records, approvals, and outcomes. Titles alone do not establish ownership; each process must identify one accountable role.

## Initial Functional Roles

- Leadership and operations
- Estimating
- Project management
- Procurement
- Delivery and logistics
- Accounting and financial control
- Systems and Brain administration

## Role Definition Standard

Each role profile should define:

- mission and expected outcomes
- responsibilities and decision authority
- required inputs and outputs
- processes owned or supported
- approval and escalation boundaries
- performance measures
- systems and records used
- backup coverage and single-person dependencies

## Required Decisions

- Confirm the current organization map.
- Identify process owners for every stage in [[NBS Process Master Map]].
- Establish an approval-authority matrix.
- Identify work that lacks backup coverage.

Use [[Role Profile Template]] for each role.
